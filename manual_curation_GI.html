Genomic Instability

  • In this bipartite graph, the genomic instability aging hallmark (centre) is connected to age-related diseases.
  • The edges represent associations that have been verified by manual curation.
  • The weight of the edges represents the scored association between the aging hallmark and the age-related disease.
  • To view manual curation results for different aging hallmarks, navigate from the drop-down list on the left.
