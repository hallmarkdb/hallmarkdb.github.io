AgingHallmarkDB

  • The nine "Hallmarks of Aging" were defined in a landmark publicaiton by Lopez-Otin et al. (2013)
  • Aging hallmarks are common denominators of the aging process. Each aging hallmark is listed below:
  • Age is a major risk factor for many chronic and deadly diseases, which are collectively known as Age-Related Diseases (ARDs). Age-related diseases were recently defined by Kuan V., Fraser HC et al. (2021).
  • Aging hallmarks are known to contribute to individual age-related diseases in humans.
  • However, not every aging hallmark is associated with every age-related disease.
  • Each aging hallmark has a greater role in the development and disordered physiology of certain age-related diseases and a lesser or no role in others.
  • This website displays the current literature evidence for the role of each aging hallmark in the development or disordered physiology of an age-related disease.
